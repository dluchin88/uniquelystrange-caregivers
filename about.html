Who We Are

Our mission is to honor the unique qualities of every person we care for. We believe that caregiving is an art that combines skill, empathy and dedication.

Our Story

Uniquely Strange Caregivers was founded by a group of healthcare and social work professionals who saw a need for more personalized home care in Houston. We’ve cared for our own loved ones and understand the importance of finding the right fit. Our services grew from a passion to celebrate every person’s individuality while helping them live life on their terms.

We’ve hand‑picked a team of caregivers who share our values. Each caregiver is vetted, trained and supported so they can deliver the high level of care your family deserves.

Our Values

Respect

We honor the dignity, independence and preferences of every person and family we serve.

Empathy

We listen with compassion and connect on a human level to provide comfort and reassurance.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of training, professionalism and reliability.

Meet the Founder

Our founder, Alex Johnson, has over 15 years of experience in nursing and elder care. After caring for his own family members, he envisioned a service that would treat every client like family. Under his guidance, Uniquely Strange Caregivers has built a reputation for quality, transparency and heartfelt service.
